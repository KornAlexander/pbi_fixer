Do you know which of your reports are still in PBIRLegacy format?

If you don't — you should. Because PBIRLegacy reports can't be edited programmatically. No fixers, no batch operations, no automated formatting. They're black boxes.

The PBI Fixer shows PBIR status for every report in your workspace:

- ⚠️ PBIRLegacy reports are flagged with warning badges in the tree
- A compact status table shows the format of all reports at a glance
- "Convert All Legacy" button for batch upgrades

And here's the thing — every single report fixer in the PBI Fixer checks for PBIR format first. PBIRLegacy? Blocked. With a clear message: "Upgrade to PBIR first."

The upgrade itself is also automated — REST round-trip via `getDefinition` → `updateDefinition`. One click per report, or batch for the whole workspace.

If you're serious about Power BI development in Fabric, PBIR is the baseline. This helps you see where you stand — and get there.

Try it: 🎯 https://actionablereporting.com/pbi-fixer
